About SKIT

Learn more about our institution, mission, and values

Our Story

Swami Keshvanand Institute of Technology (SKIT) was established with a vision to provide quality technical education and create skilled professionals who can contribute to the technological advancement of our nation.

Since our inception, we have been committed to excellence in education, research, and innovation. Our state-of-the-art facilities, experienced faculty, and industry partnerships ensure that our students receive the best possible education.

Our Vision

To be a leading institution in technical education, fostering innovation and creating globally competent engineers who contribute to society's progress.

Our Mission

To provide quality education, promote research and innovation, and develop ethical and socially responsible engineers through excellence in teaching and learning.

Our Values

Excellence

Striving for the highest standards in education and research

Integrity

Maintaining honesty and ethical practices in all our endeavors

Innovation

Encouraging creativity and innovative thinking

Collaboration

Working together to achieve common goals
