Eligibility Criteria

Academic Qualification

  • Passed 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics
  • Minimum 75% marks in 10+2 (70% for SC/ST)
  • Valid JEE Main score

Age Limit

  • General/OBC: Born on or after October 1, 1999
  • SC/ST/PwD: Born on or after October 1, 1994
  • No upper age limit for certain categories
